Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to commonly asked questions about our graphic design services.

Project timelines vary depending on the scope and complexity. A logo design typically takes 1-2 weeks, while a comprehensive brand identity package might take 3-4 weeks. We'll provide you with a specific timeline during our initial consultation based on your project requirements.

To start a project, we typically need information about your business, target audience, design preferences, competitors, project goals, and any existing brand materials. Our comprehensive briefing process ensures we capture all the necessary details to deliver designs that meet your objectives.

For most projects, we provide 2-3 initial design concepts. This allows us to explore different creative directions while ensuring each concept receives thorough attention and thought. After you select a preferred direction, we refine that concept based on your feedback.

We provide files in multiple formats suitable for various applications. For logos and graphics, you'll typically receive vector files (AI, EPS, SVG), print-ready files (PDF, TIFF), and web-optimized files (PNG, JPG). We ensure you have all the necessary formats for both digital and print usage.
