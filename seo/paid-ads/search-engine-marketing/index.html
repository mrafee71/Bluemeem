What is SEM Marketing?

SEM includes placing ads on search engine results pages (SERPs) through platforms like Google Ads to boom visibility and drive traffic to your website. It encompasses Pay-Per-Click (PPC) advertising, where advertisers bid on keywords relevant to their target audience. These ads appear above or below organic search results, marked as “Ad” to distinguish them from organic listings.

  • Boost your brand's presence on Google SERPs
  • Reach high-intent audiences searching for your services
